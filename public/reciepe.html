Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Italian pasta dish with creamy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs with Parmesan and pepper.
  4. Combine everything and serve warm.

Grilled Chicken

Juicy grilled chicken with herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Heat grill to medium-high.
  3. Grill chicken for 6-7 mins per side.
  4. Let it rest, then serve.

Vegan Salad

Fresh vegetables with tangy dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix olive oil and vinegar.
  3. Toss everything together.
  4. Serve fresh.
